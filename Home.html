Perodua Axia


A new era begins with MALAYSIAN FIRST Energy Efficient Vehicle (EEV) . The name ‘AXIA’ combines the word ‘Asia’ with the letter ‘X’, the numeral ’10′. It represents Perodua’s 10th model. This model is built to be regionally competitve. The Perodua Axia comes in four variants – Standard E, Standard G, SE and Advance – to cater to a wider range of customers. It’s built on the foundation of the 4 E’s, firmly with the needs of Malaysians mind
- An Eco, Spacious and Innovative Malaysian Compact Car .
